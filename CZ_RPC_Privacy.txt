Privacy Policy for Chroma Zero Rich Presence

Chroma Zero Rich Presence is an extension that enhances the user experience by integrating in-game activity from Chroma Zero with Discord Rich Presence. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in how your data is handled.

Chroma Zero Rich Presence only collects the following types of data:

    If you have launched, and are running the Chroma Zero application

How We Use the Data

    In-game data is only used to update your Discord Rich Presence status in real-time. This data is not shared with third parties.

Data Storage

    Local Storage: None
    Server Communication: If Chroma Zero is running, that information is used to update your Discord status. No user data is stored or sent outside of your local environment.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer any user data to outside parties. The data captured by the extension is used only for the purpose of enhancing your in-game experience with Discord Rich Presence.

Data Security

In-game status is handled via the official Discord API.
